Thor Odinson

The God of Thunder - Marvel Cinematic Universe

About Thor

Thor Odinson is the Asgardian God of Thunder, one of the most powerful superheroes in the Marvel Universe. Born as the son of Odin and Frigga, Thor wields the enchanted hammer, Mjölnir, which grants him the ability to fly, summon lightning, and unleash devastating attacks.

Powers and Abilities

Thor possesses incredible superhuman strength, speed, agility, and endurance. His control over thunder and lightning makes him a formidable opponent. As the ruler of Asgard, Thor's powers are amplified by the Odinforce, making him nearly invincible in battle.

Weapons

- Mjölnir: A magical hammer forged in the heart of a dying star. It can summon storms and always returns to Thor's hand.
- Stormbreaker: A powerful axe created to defeat Thanos, combining Asgardian magic and Groot's sacrifice.

Movies

Thor has appeared in several Marvel Cinematic Universe films, including:
- Thor (2011)
- Thor: The Dark World (2013)
- Thor: Ragnarok (2017)
- Avengers Series (2012–2019)
- Thor: Love and Thunder (2022)
